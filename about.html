ABOUT ME

 

My name is Drew Gilbert. I know my main purpose in life is to create spaces for people and excel to great heights. What I’ve done up till now is only the beginning of my journey. Growing up, my father tried his best to expose me to what life really is by never staying inside and always have something going on. I’ve been fortunate to try almost every activity that Idaho has to offer, and ultimately it has broadened my mind to believe that anything is possible if the desire is there.

 

Thank you for taking the time to examine my work and I look forward to showing you more of my progress in the near future
